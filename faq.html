寵物照顧指南 (Pet Care Guide)

餵食次數依寵物種類與年齡而異。一般來說，幼犬和幼貓每天需餵食 3～4 次，成年犬貓則建議每日餵食 2 次。請根據飼料包裝上的建議或詢問獸醫做適當調整。
Feeding frequency depends on the type and age of your pet. Puppies and kittens typically need to be fed 3–4 times a day, while adult dogs and cats are usually fed twice a day. Always check food packaging recommendations or consult a vet.

某些人類食物對狗來說是安全的，如白煮雞肉、紅蘿蔔等，但也有許多食物對狗是有毒的，如洋蔥、葡萄、巧克力等。餵食前建議先查詢或詢問獸醫。
Some human foods are safe, like plain boiled chicken or carrots. However, many foods—like onions, grapes, and chocolate—are toxic to dogs. Always check before sharing your food with your pet.

舔毛是貓咪正常的自我清潔行為，但若出現過度舔毛導致掉毛或紅腫，可能是壓力、過敏或皮膚病引起，建議儘早就醫檢查。
Grooming is normal for cats, but excessive licking that causes hair loss or redness could indicate stress, allergies, or a skin condition. It's best to consult a veterinarian.
